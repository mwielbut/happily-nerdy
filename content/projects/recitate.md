Co-founded Recitate, an education technology startup focused on improving learning outcomes through active recall and spaced repetition.

## The Vision

Recitate was built on the premise that traditional study methods are inefficient. By leveraging cognitive science principles, we created tools that helped students retain information more effectively.

## Key Learnings

- Building products for the education space
- The challenges of changing deeply ingrained study habits
- Balancing pedagogical effectiveness with user experience
