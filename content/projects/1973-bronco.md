Restoring a 1973 Ford Bronco, documenting the process of bringing a classic off-roader back to life.

## The Vehicle

The first-generation Ford Bronco (1966-1977) is an icon of American automotive design. Simple, capable, and endlessly customizable.

## The Restoration

This project involves:

- Bodywork and rust repair
- Engine and drivetrain refresh
- Interior restoration
- Modern upgrades while maintaining character

## Progress

Updates on the restoration journey will be documented here as the project progresses.
